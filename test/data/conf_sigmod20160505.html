Present CFP : 2017

Welcome to the home of the 2017 ACM SIGMOD/PODS Conference, to be held in Raleigh, North Carolina, USA, on May 14-19, 2017. The annual ACM ACM SIGMOD/PODS conference is a leading international forum for database researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to explore cutting-edge ideas and results, and to exchange techniques, tools, and experiences.
